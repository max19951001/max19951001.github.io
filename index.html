Research Interest

 I work in the field of Spatiotemporal fusion of Remote Sensing data, Downscaling of Land Surface Temperature, Mitigation of Urban Hheat Island effect. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Research on Spatio-temporal Fusion Algorithm
  • Surface temperature reconstruction with high temporal and spatial resolution in urban areas
  • The quantitative relationship between the mitigation of urban heat island effect and the green space
